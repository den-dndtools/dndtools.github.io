Epic Barbarian

(Dungeon Master's Guide v.3.5 variant, p. 207)

The Epic Barbarian is a terror to behold. The very incarnation of rage, this furious warrior can cut his opponents to ribbons with awe-inspiring ease.

Requirements

20th level barbarian


Hit die

d12

Alignment

Any chaotic

Skill points

4 + Int

Class Features

Barbarian Rage: The epic barbarian gains one use of rage per day every four levels higher than 20th (7/day at 24th, 8/day at 28th, and so on).

Trap Sense (Ex): The epic barbarian's bonus increased by +1 every three levels higher than 18th (+7 at 21st, +8 at 24th, an so on).

Damage Reduction (Ex): The epic barbarian's damage reduction increases by 1 point every three levels higher than 19th (6/- at 22nd, 7/- at 25th, and so on).

Bonus Feats: The epic barbarian gains a bonus feat every four levels higher than 20th (24th, 28th, and so on).
