Staff of the Woodlands

Magic Item
(Dungeon Master's Guide v.3.5, p. 245)

Price: 101,250 gp
Item Level: 21st
Body Slot: —
Caster Level: 13rd
Aura: Moderate;
Activation: Standard (command)
Weight: 5.0 lb.

This staff is made from oak, ash, or yew and appears to have grown naturally into its shape.

It allows use of the following spells:

  • Charm animal (1 charge)
  • Speak with animals (1 charge)
  • Barkskin (2 charges)
  • Wall of thorns (3 charges)
  • Summon nature's ally VI (3 charges)
  • Animate plants (4 charges)

The staff may be used as a weapon, functioning as a +2 quarterstaff. The staff of the woodlands also allows its wielder to pass without trace at will, with no charge cost. These two attributes continue to function after all the charges are expended.

Prerequisites: Craft Epic Magic Arms and Armor, Craft Staff , Animate Plants, Barkskin, Charm Animal, Pass Without Trace, Speak With Animals, Summon Monster VI, Wall of Thorns .

Cost to Create: 50,625 gp, 4,050 XP, 102 day(s).
